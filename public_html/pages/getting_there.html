La Cachette

Fontevraud L’Abbaye is between Saumur and Chinon, approximately 16 kilometres from each, and is a few minutes drive south from Montsoreau which is by the river Loire. Approximate driving times from various ports are as follows: Caen 3 hours, St. Malo 3 ½ hours, Cherbourg 4 ½ hours, Calais 6 hours.
There are good train services to Saumur from Paris, and there are three airports within easy reach, Angers, Poitiers, and Tours, where car hire is available.
